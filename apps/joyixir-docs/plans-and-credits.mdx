## Rate Limits

To ensure fair usage:

- **AI Messages**: 50 per day (free), unlimited (pro)
- **File Operations**: 1000 per hour
- **Export**: 10 per day (free), unlimited (pro)

<Warning>
  Exceeding rate limits will temporarily pause your access. Limits reset daily at midnight UTC.
</Warning>
